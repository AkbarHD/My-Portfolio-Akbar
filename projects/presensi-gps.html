Project Information

  • Category : Website (Laravel)
  • Find more on :  
  • Overview:WorkTrack is a GPS-based employee attendance application designed to improve efficiency, accuracy and transparency in attendance management. By utilizing GPS and facial recognition technology, WorkTrack provides a modern solution to record employee attendance in real-time, while ensuring their physical location is in accordance with company policies.
  • Objectives:
    • Attendance with GPS and Camera:
      Employees can clock in and out by utilizing cameras for facial recognition.
      The GPS location must be within a certain radius of the office for the attendance to be valid.
    • Location Radius Setting:
      Admin can specify the radius of the attendance location (for example, 100 meters from the office).
      Attendance is automatically rejected if the employee's location is outside the radius.
    • Permission and Leave Feature:
      Employees can apply for permission or leave through the permission form available in the app.
      Admin can review, approve, or reject the request.
    • Attendance History:
      Each employee has access to attendance history, including details of time in, time out, location, and status (on time or late).
      Admins can view the attendance history of all employees for reporting purposes.
    • Employee Profile Management:
      Employees can edit their profiles, such as photos, contact numbers, or other personal data.
      Admins can verify or change profile data if needed.
    • Admin Dashboard:
      Admins can view attendance recaps, tardiness statistics, and overall employee attendance percentage.
      Allows management of work schedules and attendance location radius for various office branches.
