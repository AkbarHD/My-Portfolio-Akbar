Job Vacancy App

Project Information

  • Category : Website (Laravel)
  • Find more on :  
  • Overview JobConnect is an innovative digital solution designed to bridge the gap between job seekers and employers in the modern era. Inspired by the challenges many individuals face in finding jobs that match their skills, the project aims to create an inclusive platform that simplifies the job search and hiring process. By leveraging the latest technology, JobConnect delivers a personalized experience to help users find the right career opportunities while supporting companies to find the best candidates. The focus is on creating a more connected, efficient and transparent work ecosystem.
  • Objectives:
    • Enables secure user registration and login authentication for job seekers and employers.
    • Menyediakan fitur pencarian pekerjaan berdasarkan kategori, dan keahlian.
    • Facilitates companies to post job vacancies with full descriptions and clear criteria.
    • Provides CV upload and management features for users to simplify job applications.
    • Helps individuals access career opportunities that match their skills and interests, while supporting companies to easily find top talent.
