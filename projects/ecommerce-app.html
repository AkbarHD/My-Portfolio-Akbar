  • Category : Website (Laravel)
  • Find more on :  
  • Overview: Bigbrand is an innovative e-commerce application designed to support community-based transactions with an organized hierarchy. By combining modern online shopping features and a tiered approval system, Bigbrand enables communities to manage their transactions with transparency, efficiency and structured control.
  • Objectives:
    • each community has the highest level of hierarchy and has its own roles and rights in the transaction process.
    • members can add products to cart before placing order
    • Each community has a dedicated dashboard to monitor transactions, financial reports, and performance of community members.
    • Sistem login berbasis multi-level role untuk memastikan hanya pengguna dengan akses yang sah yang dapat mengelola proses.
    • Multilevel Approval Process:
      Once a reseller places an order, the Agent receives a notification to either approve or submit the order themselves.
      If the agent chooses to move up to Distributor, the distributor receives a similar notification to decide if the order will be forwarded to the Central Admin or shipped directly.
      The final point of processing is in the hands of the Central Admin who ensures the order is fulfilled.
