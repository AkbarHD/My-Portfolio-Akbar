  • Overview: DiscussHub is an interactive digital platform designed to encourage discussion, knowledge sharing and collaboration in a variety of fields. Inspired by the need for a virtual space where people can ask questions and get answers from a diverse community, DiscussHub provides a safe, inclusive and accessible forum to exchange information. The app aims to connect individuals with relevant expertise to solve problems or answer questions effectively. here. This project build by Jetpack Compose.
  • Objectives:
    • Allows users to create accounts and ask questions in forums with relevant categories.
    • Provides a feature for other users to answer questions with clear and informative answers.
    • Menyediakan sistem voting untuk jawaban terbaik, sehingga komunitas dapat menilai relevansi dan kualitas.
    • Supports grouping discussions by topic or interest to make navigation easier.
    • Build a supportive community, where users can interact, share experiences, and broaden horizons.
