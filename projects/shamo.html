  • Overview: TechBlogPro is a modern blogging platform designed to be the center of information and discussion about the world of technology. With innovative features, TechBlogPro makes it easy for writers to manage their content, while providing a rich experience for readers. The project aims to create an ecosystem where the latest technology can be accessed, discussed and understood by everyone, both professionals and tech enthusiasts.
  • Objectives:
    • Allows users to create, edit, and manage blogs with an intuitive interface.
    • Provides special categories to make it easier to group articles, such as AI, gadgets, software, and more.
    • Supports a search feature to easily find relevant articles or topics.
    • Provides a special section for advertising, so that writers or blog managers can monetize their content.
